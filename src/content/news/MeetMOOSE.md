### Learn | Explore | Build | Connect

Are you curious about **computational neuroscience** and how to model neural systems across scales? 
If your answer is "Yes!",
come and join us for a half-day introductory webinar titled "Meet **MOOSE!**"

### Key details :
#### Duration: ~ 2.5 hours
#### Date and time : 21st March 2026, 14:00 IST
#### Platform : Zoom
#### Deadline : 28th February 2026
#### To Register [click here!](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSegcukn60-U5KOE--jj6Vn9oxI_vacNO0G1524mysnKXqmoVA/viewform)
<br>This webinar offers an introduction to MOOSE and its graphical user interface (GUI), [Jardesigner](https://jardesigner.mooseneuro.org/), designed especially for students!

Inspired by collaborative learning environments, this webinar is community-driven, beginner-friendly webinar to help students feel comfortable exploring the question "What is computational neuroscience?" and "What is MOOSE?", "How to use MOOSE?" and "How MOOSE could be helpful in your research?".

The webinar aims to provide participants with a concept-driven introduction and overview of computational neuroscience and MOOSE, supported by live demonstrations, interactive discussions, and breakout rooms to create a comfortable environment for engaging with the MOOSE team.

Come to explore, discuss, and connect!

### Schedule :

1. Welcome Note (Dr. Subhasis Ray)
2. Introduction : From molecules to MOOSE (Parita Mehta)
3. Architecture and overview of MOOSE (Ashish Anshuman)
4. Installation and Set-up of MOOSE (Jayesh Poojary)
5. From concepts to implementation : modeling with MOOSE (Gautham Dathatreyan)
6. Introduction to Jardesigner : modeling without coding (Pragathi R)
