13/**
14 * Clock now uses integral scheduling. The Clock has an array of child
15 * Ticks, each of which controls the process and reinit calls of its
16 * targets. The Clock has a minimum dt.
17 * All ticks operate with (positive) integral multiples of this,
18 * from 1 to anything. If multiple Ticks are due to go off in a given
19 * cycle, the order is from lowest to highest. Within a Tick the order
20 * of execution of target objects is undefined.
21 *
22 * The Reinit call goes through all Ticks in order.
23 */
24
