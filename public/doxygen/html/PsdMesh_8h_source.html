13/**
14 * The PsdMesh sets up the diffusion geometries for the PSD.
15 * It has to work in two contexts: first, as a PSD sitting on a spine head.
16 * Second, as a PSD sitting directly on the dendrite. In either case it
17 * is treated as a surface disc, not as a volume. On the dendrite this is
18 * a little strange conceptually (flat disc on round cylinder) but will
19 * do for now. Later can fine-tune the cap geometry.
20 *
21 * In either case, the PsdMesh is filled by a message that contains
22 * information about the matching voxel index (either on spineMesh or
23 * NeuroMesh), the coordinates, the radial vector, and the diameter.
24 * The PsdMesh does not have any internal diffusion, and it
25 * expects to pass only N to the parent dendrite or spine.
26 */
