13/**
14 * This handles synapses organized sequentially. The parent class
15 * SimpleSynHandler deals with the mechanics of data arrival.
16 * Here the algorithm is
17 * 0. Assume all synaptic input comes on a linear dendrite.
18 * 1. Maintain a history of depth D for synaptic activity. May be simplest
19 * to do as event list (sparse matrix) rather than full matrix.
20 * 2. Maintain a kernel of how to weight time and space
21 * 3. Here we have various options
22 * 3.1 At each spike event, decide how to weight it based on history.
23 * 3.2 At each timestep, compute an effective Gk and send to activation.
24 * This will be moderately nasty to compute
25 */
