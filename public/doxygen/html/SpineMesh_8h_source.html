13/**
14 * The SpineMesh sets up the diffusion geometries for dendritic spines.
15 * It is filled by a message from a NeuroMesh that contains information
16 * about the matching voxel on the NeuroMesh, and the compartment Ids for
17 * the spine shaft and head.
18 * The SpineMesh can further generate a PSD mesh with the info for the
19 * PSD geometries.
20 * This assumes that the each spine is a single voxel: single diffusive
21 * compartment, well-stirred. The shaft is treated as zero volume
22 * diffusion barrier to the dendrite.
23 * The PSD is a separate compt with its own diffusion coupling to the
24 * spine head.
25 */
