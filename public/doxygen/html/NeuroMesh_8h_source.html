13/**
14 * The NeuroMesh represents sections of a neuron whose spatial attributes
15 * are obtained from a neuronal model.
16 * Like the CylMesh, this is pseudo-1 dimension: Only the
17 * axial dimension is considered for diffusion and subdivisions. Branching
18 * is also handled.
19 *
20 *
21 * Dendritic spines typically contain different reaction systems from the
22 * dendrite, but each spine has the same reactions. So they deserve
23 * their own mesh: SpineMesh.
24 * The idea is the the SpineMesh has just the spine head compartment,
25 * which duplicate the same reactions, but does not diffuse to other
26 * spine heads.
27 * Instead it has an effective diffusion constant to the parent
28 * dendrite compartment, obtained by treating the spine neck as a
29 * diffusion barrier with zero volume.
30 */
